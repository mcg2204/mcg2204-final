My Summer Research! :)

My primary research interests in Astrophysics center around planetary science, exoplanets, and the investigation of what information about one can mean about the other. This naturally drew me to Dr. Statia Cook's research, which has centered around Neptune and various other large gas giants. A large number of exoplanets that have been discovered recently have been compared to the approixmate size and mass of Neptune, the 8th planet from our Sun. This summer, I will be conducting remote research from my home with the support of Dr. Cook. My projected centers around the atmosphere and clouds of Neptune. Using python, I will be extracting spectra taken from OSIRIS (Ohio State Infra-Red Imager), which is on the Keck telescope, to determine the properties of Neptune's clouds. From this, I hope to draw conclusions about how clouds vary with altitude, which in turn tells us about atmospheric circulation on Neptune.
